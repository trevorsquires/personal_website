Background

I'm (approximately) a np.floor((date.today() - date(1996, 1, 1)).days/365) year old data professional with a passion for solving problems. Here you'll find various information about my background and skill-sets.

Data Scientist and Mathematician

After bouncing around a few engineering disciplines, I graduated from Clemson University in 2018 with my Bachelor's in Mathematics and a minor in Computer Engineering. Four years and two graduate degrees later, I joined The Walt Disney Company's Decision Science division after graduating with my Ph.D. in Mathematics.

There, I grew from a research oriented math student into an industry minded decision scientist. I learned a ton about how, when, and when not to apply my schooling to real, large scale business problems. I developed production grade revenue management systems, managed younger coworkers, and expanded my storytelling skills into high impact, precise communication. After 3.5 years at Disney, I left in 2025 to join Sabre's revenue management arm as a Lead Data Scientist which is where I reside today.
