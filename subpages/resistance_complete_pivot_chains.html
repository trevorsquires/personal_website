Background and Description

Pokemon nuzlocking is primarily a game about preserving your Pokemon across all the battles. Pivoting is an effective way of positioning your Pokemon in a battle to minimize damage intake and maximize damage output. Here, you'll find an analysis of role-compressing Pokemon type pairings that make for effective pivots.
